About this Book

please use web-engineering for now. the rails stuff will move over here eventually

This is a textbook for the second year of a computer science or "media informatics" degree program. It builds on Web Development (which is only available in german).

This book is not a self-contained work: There are great resources for learning about git and about rails on the internet - this book tries to use these resources, and bind them together into a coherent study plan.

It was written and published by Brigitte Jellinek in the hope that it will be useful to lecturers and students in educational institutions as well as to self-directed learners on the internet.

Why Backend Development

Is there really a need for "Backend Developemnt" as a separate subject?

I would argue that web development has reached a level of complexity where it makes sense to study it's different aspectects in depth, in their own right.
